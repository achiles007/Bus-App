About

Kobe Li Tuo is a Singapore-based creative professional with a Master of Fine Arts degree from New York University. He has dedicated himself into the creative industry of Singapore for years with a proven track record in client servicing, visual developments and project management. His clients include many local and international enterprises, such as SingTel, SPH, Unilever and Audi. He is also very active in film festivals and art events as an independent animator/artist.
